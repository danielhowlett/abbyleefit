Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a gym membership to work with you?

Not necessarily! Depending on your goals and the program you choose, workouts can be tailored for at-home training with minimal equipment or structured for a full gym setting.

What is corrective exercise?

Corrective exercise involves assessing your movement patterns and identifying muscle imbalances or areas of weakness. We then use specific exercises to correct these issues, improving your posture, mobility, and reducing the risk of injury while building strength.

How do I get started with coaching?

Simply fill out the inquiry form at the bottom of this page. I'll review your goals and reach out to schedule a complimentary consultation call to discuss which option is best for you.
